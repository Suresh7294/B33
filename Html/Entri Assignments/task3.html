Chess International Day

International Chess Day, celebrated on July 20 every year, marks the founding of the International Chess Federation (FIDE) on July 20, 1924, in Paris, France. International Chess Federation (FIDE) was established to organise and oversee international chess competitions and to promote the game globally.
